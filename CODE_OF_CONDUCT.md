# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all contributors and participants in the TAKTKRONE-I project, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge that our community will be based on mutual respect, inclusivity, and collaboration.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Respectful Communication**: Using inclusive language, being welcoming to newcomers
- **Constructive Feedback**: Providing thoughtful, actionable criticism on work, not on people
- **Collaboration**: Working together toward shared goals, asking questions when unclear
- **Accountability**: Taking responsibility for our words and actions
- **Openness**: Being receptive to different viewpoints and ideas
- **Professional Conduct**: Treating all community members with dignity and respect

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments based on identity
- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning language
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, inflammatory comments, or deliberate disruption
- Publishing others' private information without consent (doxxing)
- Any conduct that creates an intimidating or hostile environment
- Abuse of power or authority within the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories, issues, and pull requests
- Discussion forums and communication channels
- Meetings and events organized by the project
- Social media representations of the project
- Any official project communications

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing **[maintainers contact]** with:

- A description of the incident
- Context and timeline
- Any relevant evidence
- Whether you'd like to remain anonymous

All reports will be treated confidentially and investigated promptly.

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- A private warning and request to stop the behavior
- Temporary muting or suspension from project spaces
- Permanent removal from the community
- Reporting to appropriate authorities if necessary

The severity of consequences will be proportional to the violation and consider the intent and impact.

### Appeal Process

If you believe you've been unfairly sanctioned, you may request a review by contacting the project maintainers with your appeal.
